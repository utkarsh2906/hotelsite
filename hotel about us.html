About us

Hotel Raj is a premier and authentic Restaurant that has been proudly serving the Pune area and beyond since 2000. Since then, our mission has been to provide high-quality food for all those that wish to combine fun and enjoyable ambiance with skillful cooking into one extraordinary dining experience.
